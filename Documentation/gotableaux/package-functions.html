registerSentence

public Sentence registerSentence(Sentence sentence)

Adds a sentence to the vocabulary, maintaining uniqueness.

If the sentence, or one of the same form is already in the vocabulary, then that sentence is returned. Otherwise the passed sentence is returned.

Parameters:
sentence - The sentence to add.
Returns:
Old or new sentence.
